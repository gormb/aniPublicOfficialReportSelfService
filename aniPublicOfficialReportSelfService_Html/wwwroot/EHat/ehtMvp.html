Einstein Tiler Terms

Hat: The base monotile shape (the 13-edge polygon) used for the aperiodic tiling.

Kite: One of the 8 congruent quadrilateral building blocks that compose a single Hat (same form, different orientation).

Meta Hat: A larger tile built by substitution from multiple Hats at one recursion level.

Meta Meta Hat: The next substitution level above Meta Hat, grouping Meta Hats into an even larger structure.
